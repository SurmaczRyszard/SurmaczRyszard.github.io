Ferrofluid speaker

During this project I had a pleasure to help my best friend, Jakub, during preparation of his diploma at Academy of Fine Arts of Łódź. It was a project of a speaker with ferromagnetic fluid which reacted to the music played at the time. 

The The design was done in a retrofuturistic style which was the main focus of the whole work of the diploma. It was, of course, done by Jakub and I was helping with the electrical and technical part of the project.

As it was a prototype, it had to be created on budget. To do so, a bluetooth speaker was used, to which we connected to a Arduino Nano clone and step-down board to power. The Arduino was gathering signal sent to the speaker from its control board, and then, after analysis of the frequencies, triggered an electromagnet to create the visual effects with the ferrofluid.
