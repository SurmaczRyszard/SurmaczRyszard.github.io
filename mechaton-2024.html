MECHATON 2024

In December of 2024, I took part in a MECHATON, which is a 48-hour engineering challenge organised by Lodz University of Technology. This time, it was done in cooperation with COMMON S.A. which is a producer of high quality equipment for gas measurement systems. 

The goal was to design a simple injection system to lubricate the bearing inside a working flow meter. It was interesting due to the high pressure inside the device that had to be overcome during application. 

I was a member of a team with 4 of my colleagues. After brainstorming, our approach was to utilise a screw and a nut design, which would apply a specific amount of oil during its movement. As I have mentioned before, the pressure inside was very high, in fact it was 110 bars. Thanks to proper design the worker does not need to apply large force and thanks to usage of the industrial valve, the pressure will not cause the oil leak into to reservoir. 

This event was an amazing opportunity to test our knowledge with a time limit. It was a thorough effort, from brainstorming and ideation to the final model, incorporating analysis, calculations, and market research.
