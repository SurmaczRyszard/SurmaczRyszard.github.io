Ryszard Surmacz

Future engineer with passion for technology and space

I am Ryszard Surmacz, a student of Lodz University of Technology, currently pursuing a Master’s in Mechanical Engineering and on double diploma programme in ECAM LaSalle in Lyon, France. I like making stuff and learning new things. Here are some of my favourite projects from university and things I made in my free time.
Check them out!

Design of a small wind tunnel for laboratory experiments

This was one of the most interesting projects I have ever worked on. This was my bachelor's thesis with a title of "Design of a small wind tunnel for laboratory experiments. 

Ferrofluid speaker

During this project I had a pleasure to help my best friend, Jakub, during preparation of his diploma at Academy of Fine Arts of Łódź. It was a project of a speaker with ferromagnetic fluid which reacted to the music played at the time. 

MECHATON 2024

In December of 2024, I took part in a MECHATON, which is a 48-hour engineering challenge organised by Lodz University of Technology. This time, it was done in cooperation with COMMON S.A. which is a producer of high quality equipment for gas measurement systems. 

RCDC 2025

In April, I took part in the RCDC challenge. It was an event organised by BEST Łódź. The goal was to build the best possible remotely controlled car from the parts provided and then race it on the course created for this occasion.
