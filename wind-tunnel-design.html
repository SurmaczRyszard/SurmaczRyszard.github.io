Design of a small wind tunnel for laboratory experiments

This was one of the most interesting projects I have ever worked on. This was my bachelor's thesis with a title of "Design of a small wind tunnel for laboratory experiments. 

It was an interesting challenge. As the name suggests, the goal was to design a wind tunnel for a university. The goal was to observe the influence of dimples on a golf ball and compare it with a regular, smooth ball. This dictated the size and conditions in the test section. 

The final design included settling chamber which stabilised the flow and prepared it for the test section after contraction, and then a fan a the end of a diffuser. The test section was designed to be made from acrylic sheets to be able to observe the sample from the desired angle.
