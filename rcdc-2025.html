RCDC 2025

In April, I took part in the RCDC challenge. It was an event organised by BEST Łódź. The goal was to build the best possible remotely controlled car from the parts provided and then race it on the course created for this occasion. 

THOur team went as 4 mechanical engineers, trying their skills in this multidisciplinary challenge. Having the most experience with programming and Arduino, I was responsible for creating software which controlled the car. It was a perfect opportunity to put my skills from previous projects into use. 

The resultant vehicle, although not the prettiest, was able to get through the course without problems. Unfortunately, we have placed just before the podium, but it was still an amazing opportunity to try ourselves in an area that is unfamiliar to our skillset, and I would love to attend the next instalment of the event.
